Can I return items in-store?

Items bought in-store can be returned for FREE to any MegaCity store. They can’t be
returned by post.

Items bought online can still be returned for FREE to your local MegaCity store – as long as
they were bought from MegaCity, not a Marketplace seller. We are unable to accept
Marketplace orders in-store.

All items being returned must have their original receipt or invoice, as well as the credit
or debit card used to make the purchase. If bought as part of a bundle, all parts of the
bundle must be returned.

Please Note: Items bought online using PayPal can be returned in-store for a
replacement, but not a refund – these must be returned by post.

If you have lost your receipt, a bank statement or Reward account history can be used
as proof of purchase.
